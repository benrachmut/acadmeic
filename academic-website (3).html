About Me

Hello! I'm Ben Rachmut, a researcher currently pursuing my post-doctoral studies at Washington University in St. Louis, focusing on the field of explainable artificial intelligence (AI). My passion lies in bridging the gap between complex algorithms and real-world applications, ensuring that the systems we build are not only effective but also transparent and understandable.

Throughout my academic journey, I earned my PhD from Ben Gurion University and developed expertise in distributed constraint optimization problems (DCOPs) and local search algorithms, contributing to the advancement of algorithms that enhance decision-making processes in dynamic environments.
